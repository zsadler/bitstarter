TruckLove Tracker helps any food lover track down thier favorite food trucks all over town. As any foodie lover knows, you walk over to where you last heard your favorite food truck was and its no where to be seen. Stop wasting that precious lunch hour and start eating! TruckLove Tracker lets you either chose a location to search or will use your current location to let you know where the closest trucks to your location.

TruckLove can be used on a desktop or any mobile device. Not only can you search for any food truck around you you can also search for a particular food truck and get its current location.

Additional features include:

  • Food Truck Bio's
  • Food Truck Menus
  • Customer Reviews
  • Directions for driving, walking & riding
